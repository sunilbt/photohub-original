About

PhotoHub brings together the people to the photographers. Photographers can regsiter online and upload their photographs for the people to view. People can select the photographers based upon their rating, minimum price or maximum price they charge for an event.

It's been designed with both photographers and people in mind, to be easily searchable and reachable throughout the region.
